Movie Review: Final Destination Bloodlines

Final Destination Bloodlines brings us back to everyone’s favorite Rube Goldberg machine of doom. This time, our unlucky lead Riley Morgan has a vision and saves his friends from a freak hot-air balloon accident (yes, really). But Death, the world’s most persistent party crasher, is not pleased. As always, the survivors drop like flies in increasingly absurd and overly complicated ways that make you wonder if Death missed its true calling as a mad engineer. With help from a sarcastic med student and a traumatized aunt who has definitely seen some things, Riley tries to outwit fate which, as usual, goes exactly as well as you’d expect in a Final Destination movie. It’s gory, it’s tense, and it’s also kind of hilarious.

Movie Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ (9/10)

Cast Members

  • Alex Monroe as Riley Morgan
  • Jade Kim as Sierra Vega
  • Malcolm Reyes as Dr. Kellan Ward
  • Lori Gates as Naomi Morgan
