The bread comes out perfectly crisp on the edges, soft in the middle, and loaded with melty cheese and garlicky flavor. Ingredients

  • 2 garlic bulbs
  • Olive oil (as needed)
  • Pinch of salt
  • Pinch of black pepper
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 1 packet active dry yeast
  • Pinch of sugar
  • 1/2 tsp vinegar
  • 1 1/2 to 2 cups bread flour
  • Mozzarella cheese

Instructions

  1. Chop off heads of both garlic bulbs. Place in ramekin and cover with olive oil, salt and black pepper.
  2. Roast at 400¬∞F for 40 minutes.
  3. To a stand mixer add 3/4 cups warm water, 1 packet active dry yeast and a pinch of sugar. Rest for 5 minutes.
  4. Add 1 1/2 tablespoons oive oil, a half teaspoon of vinegar and a pinch of salt
  5. While mixing contents of stand mixer on low/medium low add 1 1/2 to 2 cups of bread flour until dough is smooth and cohesive.
  6. Place dough in a greased bowl, cover with a damp towel and let it rest for 1 to 2 hours
  7. Transfer to a lined baking dish, cover again with the damp towel and let it rest for 15 minutes
  8. Make indents in the dough with your fingers, pour the garlic oil over the dough and squeeze the roasted garlic cloves over dough.
  9. Bake at 475¬∞F for 10 minutes, adding mozzarella half way through.
  10. After removing the dish from the oven, top with fresh chives before slicing into portions of your choice.
  11. Enjoy!ü§§
